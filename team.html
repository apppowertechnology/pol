About POLSA GRADE

POLSA GRADE is an innovative academic and student utility platform created to simplify student life through technology and digital solutions. The platform was developed to help students calculate and manage their CGPA efficiently while also providing modern student-focused tools and smart campus-centered digital features.

POLSA GRADE continues to evolve into a growing digital ecosystem focused on accessibility, innovation, student engagement, and technology-driven solutions.

Founder of POLSA GRADE

Nwigwe Goodness

Nwigwe Goodness is a student of Alvan Ikoku Federal University of Education, Department of Political Science. He currently serves as the Director of Information in his department and is passionate about technology, innovation, digital transformation, and student development.

As a software developer and creative innovator, he came up with the idea of POLSA GRADE to help students solve one of the common academic challenges of calculating and managing their CGPA efficiently.

His vision was not only to create a CGPA calculator but also to build a broader digital platform with modern features that improve student accessibility, engagement, awareness, and overall digital experience.

APP POWER TECHNOLOGY

In addition to building POLSA GRADE, Nwigwe Goodness also founded a growing technology startup known as APP POWER TECHNOLOGY. It is an innovation-driven digital startup focused on creating modern technology solutions, creative digital services, and smart web-based systems.
